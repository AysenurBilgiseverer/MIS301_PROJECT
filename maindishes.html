Falafel Recipe

Ingredients

1 cup boiled chickpeas
1 handful of dill
1 clove of garlic
1 onion
1 pinch of spice mix (as desired; thyme, sweet red
2 tablespoons of yogurt
3 tablespoons of tahin
2 drops of lemon juice

How to Make a Healthy Falafel Recipe?

We pass all the ingredients except the greens and spices through the blender. (We reserve 1 tablespoon of tahini, lemon juice and yogurt for the sauce.) Then we add the chopped greens and spices and shape them. Bake in the oven heated to 180 degrees for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, we prepare our sauce with yoghurt, tahini and lemon juice. When the falafels are cooked, we take them out of the oven and serve them with yoghurt sauce. Bon Appetit!

Roasted Mushroom Recipe

Ingredients

800 g chestnut mushrooms
3 cloves of garlic
4 tablespoons of olive oil
1 teaspoon of salt
Half a bunch of coriander

How To Make Roasted Mushroom

Chop 800 gr chestnut mushrooms into small pieces. You can use any type of mushroom you want, except chestnut mushrooms. Put the widest pan you have on the biggest burner of the stove. Turn on the heat on the highest heat and add 4 tablespoons of olive oil. After the pan is well heated, add half of the mushrooms. Do not add all the mushrooms at the same time and make sure that the mushrooms you add first are well roasted, otherwise the mushrooms may release their juices. When the mushrooms you add first are roasted, add the other half to the pan and fry well. Crush 3 cloves of garlic with the back of the knife and chop finely. After the mushrooms are roasted and change color, add the chopped garlic and 1 teaspoon of salt and fry for 1-1.5 minutes. Roughly chop half a bunch of cilantro. Turn off the stove. After the temperature of the mushroom is gone, add the coriander and mix. When you put it on the serving plate, your mushroom roast is ready and ready. Bon Appetit!

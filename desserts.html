Sugar-Free Banana Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

1 large banana
5 tablespoons of milk
1 egg
1 teaspoon of oatmeal
2 tablespoons of olive oil
1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Honey
Forest Fruits

How to Make Sugar-Free Banana Pancake

Rinse the oatmeal until it reaches the consistency of flour. Crack the egg into a large bowl and whisk well. In another bowl, mash the banana with a fork. Then add the oatmeal, banana, milk, olive oil, baking soda and one or two drops of lemon juice to the bowl with the egg that you whisked well. Mix well until the ingredients are well mixed. Let the mixture sit in the refrigerator for 30 minutes. Finally, pour the mixture into the oiled and heated pan with a ladle and cook both sides. Serve by drizzling honey on top and garnishing with forest fruits. Bon Appetit!

Sugar-Free San Sebastian Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

1 cup of almond flour
2 eggs
200 g labneh cheese
180 g cream
Half a teaspoon of carob extract
1 packet of baking powder

How To Make San Sebastian Cheesecake

How to Make Sugar-Free San Sebastian Cheesecake Beat eggs, labneh, cream and carob extract with a mixer until smooth. Add almond flour and baking powder and mix with a whisk. Put the mixture you prepared in a springform mold and bake in a preheated 180 degree oven until golden brown. (about 30-35 minutes) Slice and serve when cool. Bon Appetit!

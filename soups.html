Broccoli soup

Ingredients

500 grams of broccoli
7 glasses of water
1 tablespoon of olive oil
1 tablespoon of butter
1 tablespoon of
1.5 teaspoons of salt
1/2 teaspoon of black pepper

How to Make Broccoli Soup

Boil 500 grams of broccoli, cut into small pieces, with 7 glasses of water. After the broccoli is boiled, remove it with the help of a colander and set the water aside. Then melt 1 tablespoon of olive oil and butter in a deep saucepan. Add 1 tablespoon of flour to it and fry it until it smells and turns light color. Add the broccoli into the roasted flour little by little and the water in which you boiled the broccoli, stirring constantly with a whisk to avoid lumps. After boiling for 2-3 minutes in this way, add the boiled and drained broccoli into the water. Pass the soup through a hand blender to get a smooth consistency, and finally add 1.5 teaspoons of salt and half a teaspoon of black pepper to it.

Milk Tomato Soup Recipe

Ingredients

4 tomatoes
1 tablespoon of tomato paste
1 teaspoon of butter
1 tablespoon of olive oil
1/2 cup of milk
1 tablespoon of flour
1 teaspoon of salt
1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
5 glasses of water

How To Make Tomato Soup With Milk?

Cut the tomatoes in half and grate, paying attention to their skins. Heat the butter and olive oil in the pot in which you will cook the soup. Fry the flour with butter and olive oil for 2-3 minutes. Add the tomato puree and paste while mixing the flour roasted with the whisk. Stir, making sure there are no lumps, and slowly add the water. Add the spices and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes. Add milk and mix before serving. That's it, Bon Appetit!
